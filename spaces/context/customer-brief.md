# Customer brief

The customer builds internal tools for field operations teams. They want faster delivery of small workflow improvements without requiring every developer to be an expert in every stack.

Their priorities are:

- faster onboarding for new engineers
- more consistent implementation quality
- practical use of AI assistance in daily development
- lightweight governance that does not slow teams down

They are curious about GitHub Copilot beyond chat and completion, especially reusable context and team guidance.
